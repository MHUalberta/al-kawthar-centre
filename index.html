Al-Kawthar Centre for Learning Arabic, Qur'an, and Islamic Studies.

Al-Kawthar Centre is a private institution that offers Arabic and Qur’an classes for all ages.

Our mission: to make students learn Arabic in fun and creative ways and apply its skills in their daily lives.

Our centre offers:
  • In-person classes for kids and youth.
  • Online classes for kids, youth, and adults.
  • Modern educational methods using a variety of useful resources.
  • Free trial classes.
  • Affordable fees.

Our Services

  • Arabic

    At Al-Kawthar Centre, ongoing assessments and weekly assignments are assigned to students in Arabic

  • Qur’an

    At Al-Kawthar Centre, students recite and memorize the Qur’an every week in Qur’an class.

  • Islamic Studies

    At Al-Kawthar Centre, students learn about the stories of Prophets, the pillars of Islam and Iman, and other important Islamic topics in Islamic Studies.
